Facts about CAMEL

  • Camel's ears are furry.
  • They can reach 7 feet in height and weigh up to 1500 pounds.
  • Their eyes have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand to enter their eyes.

Facts about CHAMELEON
  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
  • They come in a lot of different shapes and sizes.
  • They have extremely powerful tongues.

Facts about PIGEON
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  • They are highly sociable animals.
  • They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph.

Facts about BEAR
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.
  • They are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Some species of Asiatic bear build nests in the trees.
